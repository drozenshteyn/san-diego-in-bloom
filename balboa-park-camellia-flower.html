White Camellia (Camellia japonica)

This elegant white camellia blooms quietly beneath the trees, its soft petals glowing in the filtered garden light. Photographed in the Japanese Friendship Garden at Balboa Park, the flower reflects the peaceful spirit of the space—a place designed to celebrate harmony, nature, and cultural connection. Nestled among winding paths and koi ponds, this camellia stands out with its simple beauty and graceful form.

Camera: NikonD7500

Lens: Pentacon 50mm f/1.8

Lens effect: Soft, painterly bokeh created in-camera using vintage manual lens.
