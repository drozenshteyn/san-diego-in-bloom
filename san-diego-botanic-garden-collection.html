San Diego Botanic Garden

The San Diego Botanic Garden is a vibrant oasis showcasing an incredible variety of plants from around the world. I’m continually inspired by the garden’s diverse landscapes — from fragrant Mediterranean herbs to colorful desert succulents and lush tropical blooms.

This collection highlights the garden’s rich textures and vivid colors, featuring delicate wildflowers, towering bamboo groves, and exotic plants that create a peaceful, ever-changing tapestry of life. I especially enjoy photographing close-up details that reveal the unique beauty and personality of each species.

Through a soft-focus style created with vintage lenses and an emphasis on natural light, I aim to evoke the gentle atmosphere and quiet elegance of the garden. Each image offers a glimpse into the calm and wonder cultivated in this special corner of San Diego. To explore more about the garden and its inspiring plant collections, visit the San Diego Botanic Garden website.
