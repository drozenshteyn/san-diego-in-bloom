Bloom Journal

Welcome to the Bloom Journal — a photography blog capturing San Diego's vibrant wildflowers, blooming gardens, scenic trails, and native plants. Here I share seasonal flower features, photo essays, and field notes from hikes and visits to nature preserves across the region. Whether you're a flower lover, nature walker, or photography enthusiast, there's something blooming here for you.
