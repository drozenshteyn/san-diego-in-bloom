Water Lily (Nymphaea sp.)

Floating gently on the still surface of the pond, this water lily glows with soft pink petals and a golden center. Photographed at the iconic Lily Pond in Balboa Park, the flower captures a moment of calm and quiet beauty. The pond, originally built in 1915 for the Panama-California Exposition, has become a beloved spot in San Diego, known for its blooming lilies and peaceful reflections. This particular bloom stands out against the soft ripples of the water, offering a glimpse into the timeless charm of the park's gardens.

Camera: NikonD7500

Lens: Pentacon 50mm f/1.8

Lens effect: Soft focus and gentle bokeh created in-camera using vintage manual lens.
