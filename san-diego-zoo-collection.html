San Diego Zoo

The San Diego Zoo is world-renowned not only for its incredible animals but also for its vast and diverse botanical collection. As a nature photographer, I’m captivated by the lush greenery and vibrant plant life that create a living backdrop throughout the zoo’s many habitats.

One of my favorite places to photograph is the Fern Canyon, a peaceful, shaded retreat where towering ferns and tropical palms form a magical, almost otherworldly landscape. This collection focuses on the rich textures and delicate details of the zoo’s extensive flora — from the graceful ferns to the striking bird-of-paradise and colorful hibiscus flowers that brighten the gardens.

With vintage lenses and natural light, I aim to capture the serene beauty and subtle moods of these green spaces, revealing the quiet wonders that often go unnoticed amid the zoo’s lively environment. Each photo is a celebration of the harmony between plants and wildlife in this iconic San Diego destination. To learn more about the amazing plant life and habitats, visit the San Diego Zoo website.
